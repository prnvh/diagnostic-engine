Current scope

The current registry covers four knee conditions plus a safety escalation layer.

The page is tuned for a focused knee intake flow: free-text opening story, one-question form rounds, and a structured shortlist only after enough evidence has been earned.

Modeled nodes

What the engine reasons about today

ACL tear

Pivot injury, pop, rapid swelling, instability, and trust loss in sport-like movement.

Meniscal tear

Inner or outer joint-line pain, catching or locking, twisting history, and range-of-motion loss.

Patellofemoral pain syndrome

Front-of-knee pain, stairs, squatting, sitting with the knee bent, and activity-related pain.

Knee osteoarthritis

Longer timeline, stiffness after rest, crepitus, walking discomfort, and load-related symptoms.

Interpretation

How to read the outcome

Fit score, not diagnosis

The shortlist ranks the best registry fits from the evidence gathered so far.

Fallback means bounded confidence

If confidence is not earned, the engine stops rather than pretending certainty it does not have.

Safety overrides ranking

Hot red knee, fever, major trauma, deformity, and similar patterns trigger the safety path first.

Ledger stays inspectable

Every major session event can be refreshed and reviewed from the same page.
